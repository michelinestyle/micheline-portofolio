wHeelhub History

The Beginning (1995)

Wheel Hub opened its first showroom in 1995, with a vision to revolutionize the automotive retail industry. Located in a modest storefront, our founders were driven by a passion for cars and a commitment to providing customers with high-quality vehicles and exceptional service.

Rapid Expansion (2000)

By the year 2000, Wheel Hub had rapidly expanded, opening multiple showrooms across the United States. Our reputation for excellence in customer service and a wide selection of vehicles helped us become a trusted name in automotive retail.

Embracing Innovation (2010)

In 2010, Wheel Hub embraced new technological advancements, integrating sophisticated diagnostic tools and offering cutting-edge automotive accessories. This commitment to innovation ensured our customers had access to the latest in automotive technology.

Award Winning Service (2015)

Our dedication to customer satisfaction was recognized in 2015 when Wheel Hub received several prestigious awards for outstanding service and business excellence. These accolades solidified our standing as a leader in the automotive industry.

Community Engagement (2020)

Wheel Hub has always believed in giving back to the community. In 2020, we launched several initiatives aimed at supporting local communities, including educational programs and partnerships with charitable organizations.

Launching the Online Platform (2024)

Now, in 2024, Wheel Hub is proud to unveil our state-of-the-art online platform. This virtual showroom brings the exceptional Wheel Hub experience to your fingertips, offering a seamless and immersive way to explore our wide range of vehicles and accessories.
